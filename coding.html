top websites

Here is the list of top coding platforms where you can learn and practice coding.

Hackerrank

HackerRank is a technology company that focuses on competitive programming challenges for both consumers and businesses, where developers compete by trying to program according to provided specifications.

Hackerearth

HackerEarth is a startup technology company based in Bangalore, India that provides recruitment solutions,competitive programming platform which supports over 32 programming languages.

Codechef

CodeChef is a non-profit educational initiative of Directi. It aims to provide a platform for students, young software professionals to practice, hone their programming skills through online contests

TopCoder

Topcoder is a crowdsourcing company with a global open community of designers, developers, data scientists, and competitive programmers.

Spoj

SPOJ (Sphere Online Judge) is an online judge system with over 315,000 registered users and over 20,000 problems.

Techgig

Techgig is India's Largest online Tech Community, where you can learn, update your skills, compete with fellow techies and get your dream job.
